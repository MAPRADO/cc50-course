Know our services!

Veterinary Medicine

A PET veterinarian is a professional who cares for the health and welfare of pets. He or she may work in veterinary clinics, veterinary hospitals, pet shops, or serve at home. The veterinarian is responsible for performing consultations, examinations, and surgeries on pets such as dogs, cats, birds, and rodents. In addition, he can advise owners on proper nutrition and the necessary care to maintain the animal's health.

Bathing and Grooming

Bathing and grooming are services offered in pet shops to maintain the hygiene and appearance of pets. Bathing is important to remove dirt and odors from the animal, while grooming helps to keep the coat more beautiful and healthy. In addition, grooming can help prevent skin problems and parasites.

Bathing and Hydration

Hydration is an important process to maintain the health of pets' coats. It starts with proper bathing frequency, with the correct use of shampoos and conditioners on dogs. Depending on each pet's needs taking into account factors such as lifestyle, coat type, and special dermatological needs, it may be complemented with the use of other products.

Dog Taxi

Taxi Dog is a specialized transportation service for pets. Nowadays, both self-employed professionals and pet shops are offering this service that represents more convenience for both owners and pets. Despite the name, it doesn’t only transport dogs and can transport any type of pet. It is important to define in advance which types of animals can be transported in your taxi dog.
